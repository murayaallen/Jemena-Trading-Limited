Our Story

Jemena Trading Limited was born in 2020, a year defined by global upheaval and extraordinary challenges. In the face of unprecedented demand for critical chemical products, the company emerged as a solution-driven force, answering the call for industries in desperate need. Founded with a vision to provide reliable, high-quality chemical solutions during a time of crisis, Jemena Trading Limited quickly established itself as a lifeline for essential sectors. What began as a determined effort to meet immediate needs has grown into a dynamic enterprise that now serves a vast array of industries, including manufacturing, mining, water treatment, and food processing.

Driven by innovation, integrity, and an unyielding commitment to excellence, the company has built its reputation by partnering with trusted global suppliers and ensuring its products meet the highest international standards.
